~:KHAN RESTAURANT:~

Teste the Royal Flavors

~:SHAHI KHEER:~

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice – 1/4 cup (washed and soaked for 30 minutes)
  • Full-fat milk – 1 liter
  • Sugar – 1/2 to 3/4 cup (adjust to taste)
  • Ghee (clarified butter) – 1 tablespoon
  • Cardamom powder – 1/2 teaspoon
  • Saffron strands – a few (soaked in 2 tablespoons warm milk)
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios) – 2–3 tablespoons
  • Rose water – 1 teaspoon (optional)
