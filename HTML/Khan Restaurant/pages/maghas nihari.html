Teste the Royal Flavors

~:MAGHAS NIHARI:~

Ingredients:

  • Goat brain (Maghaz) – 2 to 3 (cleaned, membrane removed)
  • Oil/Ghee – ½ cup
  • Onions – 2 medium (finely sliced)
  • Ginger garlic paste – 2 tbsp
  • Wheat flour (atta) – 2 tbsp (roasted, for thickening)
  • Nihari masala – 3 to 4 tbsp (ready-made, or homemade mix)
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Water – 4 to 5 cups
  • For Garnish:
  • Fresh coriander – chopped
  • Green chilies – sliced
  • Ginger – cut into thin strips
  • Lemon – wedges
