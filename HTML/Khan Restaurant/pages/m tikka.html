Teste the Royal Flavors

~:MUTTON TIKKA:~

Ingredients:

  • Mutton boneless – ½ kg (cut into cubes)
  • Yogurt – ½ cup
  • Ginger garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Lemon juice – 2 tbsp
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp (or to taste)
  • Crushed red chili – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Garam masala powder – 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tsp
  • Cumin powder – 1 tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Oil – 2 tbsp
  • Skewers – as required
