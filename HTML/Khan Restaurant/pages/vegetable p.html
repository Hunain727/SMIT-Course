Teste the Royal Flavors

~:VEGETABLE PIZZA:~

Ingredients:

  • For the Dough:
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water (110°F / 45°C)
  • For the Sauce:
  • 1 cup tomato sauce or pizza sauce
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
