  • 500g ground beef (minced meat)

  • 1 tsp paprika (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil (for frying or grilling)
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Extra BBQ sauce (for topping)
