Teste the Royal Flavors

~:BEEF BIRYANI:~

Ingredients:

  • Beef – ½ kg (with bones or boneless)
  • Rice – ½ kg (Basmati or Sella)
  • Onions – 2 large (sliced)
  • Tomatoes – 2 medium (chopped)
  • Yogurt – ½ cup
  • Ginger garlic paste – 2 tbsp
  • Green chilies – 3–4 (chopped)
  • Fresh coriander – ½ cup (chopped)
  • Fresh mint – ½ cup (chopped)
  • Red chili powder – 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tbsp
  • Garam masala powder – 1 tsp
  • Biryani masala – 2 tbsp (optional)
  • Lemon juice – 2 tbsp
  • Oil or ghee – ½ cup
  • Salt – to taste
  • Whole spices (1 cinnamon stick, 4–5 cloves, 4–5 black pepper, 2 black cardamoms, 2 bay leaves)
