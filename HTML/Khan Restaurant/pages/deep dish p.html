~:DEEP DISH PIZZA:~

Ingredients:

  • 3 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ tsp sugar
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ cup warm water (110°F / 45°C)
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil (or olive oil)
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • For the Sauce:
  • 2 cups canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp olive oil
