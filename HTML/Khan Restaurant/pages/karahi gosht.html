~:KHAN RESTAURANT:~

Teste the Royal Flavors

~:KARAHI GOSHT:~

Ingredients:

  • ½ kg mutton or beef (with bones, cut into pieces)
  • 2 medium onions (finely sliced)
  • 3 medium tomatoes (chopped or blended)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ½ cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (roasted & crushed)
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper (crushed)
  • Salt to taste
  • 3–4 green chilies (whole or sliced)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • ½ cup yogurt (optional, for rich taste)
