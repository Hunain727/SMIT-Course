~:KHAN RESTAURANT:~

Teste the Royal Flavors

~:BEEF NIHARI:~

Ingredients:

  • Beef shank with bone marrow (nalli) – 1 kg
  • Onion – 2 medium (sliced)
  • Ginger paste – 1 tbsp
  • Garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Wheat flour (atta) – 3 tbsp (for thickening)
  • Oil or ghee – 1 cup
  • Salt – to taste
  • Water – as required
  • Whole Spices (dry roast and grind into powder):
  • Fennel seeds – 1 tbsp
  • Coriander seeds – 1 tbsp
  • Cumin seeds – 1 tsp
  • Black pepper – 1 tsp
  • Green cardamom – 4
  • Cloves – 4
  • Cinnamon stick – 1 small piece
