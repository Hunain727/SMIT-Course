~:KHAN RESTAURANT:~

Teste the Royal Flavors

~:SHAHI NIHARI:~

Ingredients:

  • Beef (shank or stew meat) – 1 kg
  • Onion – 2 (finely sliced)
  • Ginger paste – 1 tbsp
  • Garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Wheat flour – 3 tbsp (roasted)
  • Yogurt – ½ cup
  • Oil/Ghee – 1 cup
  • Salt – to taste
  • Water – as required
  • Whole spices (for nihari masala):
  • Fennel seeds – 1 tbsp
  • Coriander seeds – 1 tbsp
  • Black pepper – 1 tsp
  • Cloves – 5–6
  • Green cardamom – 4
  • Black cardamom – 1
  • Cinnamon stick – 1 inch
  • Nutmeg – a pinch
  • Mace – a small piece
  • Red chili powder – 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
