Teste the Royal Flavors

~:HARI MIRCH TIKKA:~

Ingredients:

  • Chicken – 500 g (small pieces, boneless or with bone)
  • Green chilies – 6–8 (thick, less spicy ones)
  • Yogurt – ½ cup
  • Ginger garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Lemon juice – 2 tbsp
  • Green chili paste – 1 tbsp (optional, for extra spice)
  • Fresh coriander leaves – 2 tbsp (chopped)
  • Salt – 1 tsp (or to taste)
  • Black pepper – 1 tsp
  • Cumin powder – ½ tsp
  • Oil – 2 tbsp
  • Butter – for basting (optional)
