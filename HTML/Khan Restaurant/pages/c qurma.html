Teste the Royal Flavors

~:CHICKEN QURMA:~

Ingredients:

  • Chicken – 1 kg (cut into pieces)
  • Yogurt – 1 cup
  • Onion – 2 large (finely sliced)
  • Garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Ginger paste – 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies – 3–4 (optional, slit)
  • Oil or ghee – ½ cup
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tsp
  • Garam masala – 1 tsp
  • Whole spices:
  • Cinnamon stick – 1
  • Cloves – 3–4
  • Cardamom – 3–4
  • Salt – to taste
  • Fresh cream – 2–3 tbsp (optional)
  • Fresh coriander – for garnish
