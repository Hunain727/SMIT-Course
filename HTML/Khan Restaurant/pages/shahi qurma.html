~:KHAN RESTAURANT:~

Teste the Royal Flavors

~:SHAHI QURMA:~

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg mutton or chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt (whisked)
  • 2 large onions (finely sliced)
  • 3–4 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream
  • 1/4 cup almonds (soaked and ground into a paste)
  • 1/4 cup cashews (optional, ground into a paste)
  • 2–3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and fried onions for garnish
