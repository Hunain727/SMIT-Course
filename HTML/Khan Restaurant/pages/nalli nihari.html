~:KHAN RESTAURANT:~

Teste the Royal Flavors

~:NALLI NIHARI:~

Ingredients:

  • For Meat:
  • Beef shank with bone marrow (nalli) – 1 kg
  • Onion – 2 medium (sliced)
  • Oil or ghee – 1 cup
  • Salt – to taste
  • Water – as required
  • For Paste:
  • Ginger paste – 1 tbsp
  • Garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • For Nihari Masala (Spice Mix):
  • Fennel seeds – 1 tbsp
  • Coriander seeds – 1 tbsp
  • Cumin seeds – 1 tsp
  • Black pepper – 1 tsp
  • Green cardamom – 4
  • Cloves – 4
  • Cinnamon stick – 1 small piece
