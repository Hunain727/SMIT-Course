~:BOMBAY MUTTON BIRYANI:~

Ingredients:

  • Mutton (goat meat) – 1 kg (with bones)
  • Basmati rice – 700 g
  • Onions – 3 large (thinly sliced)
  • Tomatoes – 3 medium (chopped)
  • Yogurt – 1 cup
  • Ginger garlic paste – 3 tbsp
  • Green chilies – 4–5 (slit)
  • Fresh coriander – ½ cup (chopped)
  • Fresh mint – ½ cup (chopped)
  • Lemon juice – 2 tbsp
  • Red chili powder – 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tbsp
  • Cumin powder – 1 tsp
  • Garam masala powder – 1 tsp
  • Biryani masala – 2 tbsp (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Whole spices (2 bay leaves, 1 cinnamon stick, 5 cloves, 5–6 black pepper, 3 green cardamoms, 2 black cardamoms, 1 star anise)
  • saffron – a few strands (soaked in warm milk) OR yellow food color
  • Oil or ghee – 1 cup
  • Salt – to taste
