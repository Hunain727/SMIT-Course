Teste the Royal Flavors

~:GREEN CHILLI KARAHI:~

Ingredients:

  • ½ kg chicken (small pieces)
  • 8–10 green chilies (whole or sliced)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (chopped or blended)
  • 1 medium onion (finely sliced)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ½ cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (roasted & crushed)
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes (optional, for extra spice)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream or yogurt (optional, for taste)
