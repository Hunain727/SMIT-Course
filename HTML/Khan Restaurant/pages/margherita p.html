~:MARGHERITA PIZZA:~

Ingredients:

  • For the Dough:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • For the Topping:
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (or tomato puree with a pinch of salt & oregano)
  • 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • Optional: Oregano or chili flakes
